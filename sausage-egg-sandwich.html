Egg & Sausage Grilled Sandwich

This egg and sausage grilled cheese sandwich is an easy breakfast sandwich, but you can make it any time of the day. This grilled cheese sandwich recipe is very delicious and high in protein!

prep time

5 mins.

cook time

10 mins.

serving

2 servings.

Tags : breakfast side food

instructions

step 1

Scramble the eggs and cook the sausage in the same pan.

step 2

In a small bowl or jar mix tomato paste with garlic and 1 tsp olive oil.

step 3

Spread the tomato paste on 2 slices of bread. Top with the eggs and mozzarella cheese. Top each with another slice of bread.

step 4

Toast in a pan on both sides with olive oil until golden brown.

ingredients

2 eggs

1/2 cup mozzarella

1 tbsp tomato paste

1 garlic clove

1/2 cup sausage

1 jalapeno

2 tsp olive oil

4 slices bread

tools

Oven

Pan
